Eris

Chaos, in the Browser

Three interactive demos of deterministic chaos — systems with no randomness at all that are nonetheless impossible to predict. From the period-doubling route into chaos and the Lyapunov exponent that measures it, to the real chaotic tumbling of Saturn's moon Hyperion. Rebuilt from my undergraduate computational-physics coursework.
